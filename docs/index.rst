How to Download and Install Peacock TV App
==========================================

Peacock TV is a popular streaming platform owned by NBCUniversal that offers a wide range of movies, TV shows, live sports, news, and exclusive Peacock Originals. The platform is available on many devices, including smart TVs, streaming players, gaming consoles, smartphones, and web browsers.

Downloading and installing the Peacock TV app is quick and simple. Once installed, you can sign in or activate your account to begin streaming your favorite content.

This guide explains how to download and install the Peacock TV app on different devices step by step.

How to Download Peacock TV App on Smart TV
------------------------------------------

Follow these steps to install the Peacock TV app on your smart TV:

1. Turn on your smart TV and connect it to the internet.
2. Open the **App Store** or **App Marketplace** on your TV.
3. Use the search feature and type **Peacock TV**.
4. Select the official Peacock TV application from the search results.
5. Click **Install** or **Download**.
6. Wait for the installation to complete.
7. Open the Peacock TV app from your TV's app list.

Once the app opens, you can sign in or activate your Peacock account.

How to Install Peacock TV on Roku
---------------------------------

If you are using a Roku device, follow these steps:

1. Turn on your Roku device.
2. Navigate to the **Roku Channel Store**.
3. Select **Search Channels**.
4. Type **Peacock TV** in the search bar.
5. Choose the Peacock TV app.
6. Click **Add Channel**.
7. Wait for the channel to install.

After installation, launch the app and sign in to your Peacock account.

How to Download Peacock TV on Amazon Fire TV
--------------------------------------------

To install Peacock TV on an Amazon Fire TV device:

1. Turn on your Fire TV device.
2. Open the **Amazon App Store**.
3. Use the search option and type **Peacock TV**.
4. Select the official app from the results.
5. Click **Download** or **Get**.
6. Wait for the installation process to finish.
7. Open the Peacock TV app.

You can now sign in or activate your device.

How to Install Peacock TV on Apple TV

Follow these steps to download the Peacock TV app on Apple TV:

1. Turn on your Apple TV.

3. Search for **Peacock TV**.
4. Select the official application.
5. Click **Install**.

Sign in using your Peacock account to start streaming.

How to Download Peacock TV on Android Devices

Android users can install the Peacock TV app from the Google Play Store.

How to Install Peacock TV on iPhone or iPad
-------------------------------------------

To download Peacock TV on an iOS device:

1. Open the **Apple App Store**.
2. Search for **Peacock TV**.
3. Tap the official Peacock app.
4. Tap **Get** to download.
5. Wait for the installation to complete.
6. Open the app and sign in to your account.

How to Sign In After Installing Peacock TV
------------------------------------------

Once the app is installed, you can sign in to your account.

Steps:

1. Open the Peacock TV app.
2. Select **Sign In**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Enter your password.
5. Click **Continue**.

Some devices may display an activation code that you must enter on the Peacock activation website to link your device.
